About

Bristol City Libraries:

Nurturing Minds, Building Communities

At Bristol City Libraries, we believe in the power of libraries to transform lives and build stronger communities. Our mission is to provide accessible and inclusive library services that inspire a love for reading, support learning, and foster a sense of belonging. Discover a world of knowledge, imagination, and exploration at Bristol City Libraries. Whether you're an avid reader, a student, or someone looking to dive into the fascinating realm of literature, our libraries are here to serve you. Explore our vast collection of books, digital resources, and engaging events. Join us on a journey of learning and discovery..
